Social Analytics

We track performance across platforms, analyse audience behaviour, and optimise campaigns to achieve your marketing goals. Social Analytics provides actionable insights that help you make data-driven decisions and improve ROI for all your social media efforts.

This strategy ensures every campaign is evaluated, refined, and aligned with business objectives, maximising growth and engagement.

Performance Tracking:

  • Monitor engagement metrics such as likes, comments, shares, clicks, and saves across all platforms.
  • Measure reach, impressions, and audience growth to evaluate campaign effectiveness.
  • Track website traffic, leads, and conversions generated through social media campaigns.

Analysis & Optimisation:

  • Analyse audience behaviour to understand preferences, peak engagement times, and content performance.
  • Use data-driven insights to adjust strategies, creatives, and targeting for improved results.
  • Continuously optimise campaigns to maximise ROI and achieve defined marketing objectives.

Our Approach

Our approach to Social Analytics ensures that every campaign is measurable and optimised for maximum impact. Here's a typical workflow we follow:

Data Collection & Tracking:

  • Collect performance data from all social media platforms and integrate with analytics tools for comprehensive tracking.

Insight Analysis:

  • Analyse audience behaviour, engagement patterns, and content performance to identify areas for improvement.

Optimisation & Reporting:

  • Refine campaigns based on analytics, run A/B tests, and provide clear reports to stakeholders for informed decision-making.
